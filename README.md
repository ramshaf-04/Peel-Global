# Peer Global

Peer Global is a web-based learning platform focused on high-school curriculum for web development. We provide a structured, career-minded pathway from fundamentals to real-world projects, led by exceptional instructors who combine industry experience with classroom-focused teaching.

## Mission
To empower high-school-aged learners with the practical web development skills and professional habits necessary to build meaningful projects and pursue further education or early-career opportunities.

## Who this is for
Primary target audience: male high-school students who are interested in learning web development in a focused, instructor-led environment. Our programs are designed for beginners and early intermediates who want clear guidance, hands-on practice, and mentor support.

## What you’ll learn
- Core building blocks: HTML, CSS, and responsive design
- JavaScript fundamentals and DOM manipulation
- Modern front-end frameworks and tooling (component design, state management)
- Backend basics: APIs, databases, and simple server patterns
- Practical workflows: version control basics, debugging, and deployment
- Portfolio development: guided capstone projects and real-world assignments

## Learning approach
- Structured curriculum: modules are sequenced to build skills incrementally.
- Instructor-led sessions: live lessons and Q&A with experienced instructors who bring real-world perspectives.
- Hands-on projects: applied assignments and a capstone project to showcase skills.
- Assessments & feedback: regular checks for understanding and constructive instructor feedback to keep learners on track.

## Why Peer Global stands out
- Excellent instructors: carefully selected educators who combine teaching skill with practical experience.
- Clear structure: focused learning pathways that reduce ambiguity and keep progress steady.
- Results-oriented: emphasis on producing portfolio-ready work that demonstrates competency.

## How to get started
1. Choose a learning track and enroll through our platform.
2. Attend live sessions and complete module assignments.
3. Participate in mentorship and feedback cycles.
4. Build and publish your capstone project to showcase your skills.

## Outcomes
Graduates will have a portfolio of projects, practical web development experience, and the foundations required for internships, advanced courses, or self-directed projects.
